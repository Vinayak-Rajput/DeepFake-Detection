Terms of Use & Information

Welcome to the Deepfake Media Detector application. By registering an account and using this service, you agree to the following terms:

Data Usage Policy

  • Files uploaded are processed for the sole purpose of deepfake detection analysis.
  • Uploaded files are stored temporarily on the server during processing and may be deleted shortly after.
  • If "Enable Blockchain Logging" is selected, the SHA-256 hash of the file, the prediction result (Real/Fake), the confidence score, a timestamp, and (if applicable) the XAI explanation filename are logged immutably on a connected blockchain (currently a local Ganache test network).
  • No personally identifiable information (other than your username, if associated with the transaction) is stored alongside the detection results on the blockchain.

Accuracy Disclaimer

  • The AI models used for detection are experimental and may produce inaccurate results (false positives or false negatives).
  • The results provided should be considered indicative and not definitive proof of authenticity or manipulation. Always use critical judgment.

User Responsibility

  • You are responsible for ensuring you have the necessary rights to upload and analyze the media files you submit.
  • Do not upload illegal, sensitive, or confidential material.

Why Verity Lens?

Dual AI Analysis

Uses a CNN (Xception) for high-speed image analysis and a powerful CNN+LSTM model to detect temporal inconsistencies in videos.

Immutable Ledger

Every new analysis can be logged to a blockchain (Ganache), creating a tamper-proof, verifiable record of the result tied to the file's hash.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Don't just get an answer, get an explanation. Our app uses LIME to generate a heatmap overlay, showing *why* an image was flagged as real or fake.
